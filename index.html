NFTs Documentation

NFTs has become a hot topic as of late 2021. Before we explore deeper about NFTs, you should know that it's not a new thing it's been around for years... So, what are NFTs? NFT stand for Non-fungible token. A non-fungible token is a non-interchangeable unit of data stored on a blockchain, a form of digital ledger, that can be sold and traded. Types of NFT data units may be associated with digital files such as photos, videos, and audio.

To simplify things up, a non-fungible token (NFT) is a unique digital asset that represents ownership of real-world items like art, video clips, music, and more. NFTs use the same blockchain technology that powers cryptocurrencies, but they're not a currency.
